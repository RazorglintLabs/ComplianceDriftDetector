# Refund and Cancellation Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

## 1. Subscription Cancellation

Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time.

Cancellation stops future billing. Access to updates, support, assisted interpretations, and license rights may end at the end of the current paid billing period.

## 2. Starter Trial

The Starter License may include a 7-day trial when offered through Stripe. If the customer does not cancel before the trial ends, monthly billing begins automatically.

## 3. Refunds

Because ComplianceDriftDetector is digital software and access is provided immediately, completed subscription payments are generally non-refundable.

Refunds may be considered case-by-case if:

- A duplicate payment was made
- A technical billing error occurred
- The customer was charged after a confirmed cancellation request
- Access was not delivered due to an error on our side

## 4. Assisted Review Refunds

One-time assisted report reviews are refundable only before review work begins.

Once review work has started or a report interpretation has been delivered, assisted review payments are generally non-refundable.

## 5. No Refund for Misuse

Refunds are not provided when a customer submits unsupported, incomplete, non-redacted, unauthorized, or unsuitable data, or expects the product to provide certification, legal advice, audit approval, or guaranteed compliance.
